## **Step-by-Step Score Prediction Approach**
### **1. Factors Affecting Score**
#### **Key Components**
1. **Current Run Rate (CRR)**
   - \( \text{CRR} = \frac{\text{Current Score}}{\text{Overs Played}} \)
   - Helps predict the **base score**.
  
2. **Middle Overs Performance (10-15)**
   - Usually slower than the powerplay.
   - Average: **7-9 runs per over**.

3. **Death Overs Acceleration (16-20)**
   - Teams score more aggressively.
   - Average: **10-12 runs per over**.

4. **Wickets Lost Impact**
   - More wickets down ‚Üí Less acceleration.
   - Subtract **5 runs per lost wicket**.

5. **Pitch Conditions**
   - **Flat pitch (Mumbai, Bangalore)** ‚Üí **+10 to +15 runs**.
   - **Bowling pitch (Chennai, Delhi)** ‚Üí **-10 to -20 runs**.

6. **Weather Impact**
   - **Dew (favoring batsmen)** ‚Üí **+10 runs**.
   - **Rain/humidity (slows batting)** ‚Üí **-10 runs**.

7. **Bowling Strength**
   - **Good bowlers (Economy < 7, Strike Rate < 20)** ‚Üí **-10 runs**.
   - **Expensive bowlers (Economy > 9)** ‚Üí **+10 runs**.

8. **Batsman Strike Rate**
   - **SR > 140** ‚Üí **+10 runs**.
   - **SR < 120** ‚Üí **-10 runs**.
